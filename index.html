Do you know the significance behind the celebration of Holi ?

Holi represents the arrival of spring and the triumph of good over evil. It is also said to be the enactment of a game the Hindu god Lord Krishna played with his consort Radha and the gopis.

Holi is celebrated during the Phalguna month, which typically falls in the month of March. Holi 2021 Date in India: Called the Festival of Colours, Holi is widely celebrated across India.This year Holi was celebrated in a grand fashion throughout the nation on March 29,2021
